# Animal-Bites

This Library was created by Harley Hannahs and Orville Anderson in 2026, for CSci 2601 at the University of Minnesota Morris.

This project explores the [Animal Bites dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/rtatman/animal-bites). This dataset contains 9,000+ entries of bite incidents near Louisville, Kentucky from the Louisville Metro Department of Public Health and Wellness. Data was collected from 1985 to 2017.

A supplementary library `` explores some of the function used in exploring the Animal Bites dataset.

This project aims to answer the following questions:

* Least common animal to be bitten by?
* What animal bite is most likely to result in rabies on average?
* Type of animal most likely to be caught after a bite?
* What day of the week or year has the most bites on average? What time of day has the most bites?
* Most recent animal bite?
* What is the average time between time of bite and time head was sent?
* What places were people bitten besides head and body?
